What We Do

Infirmary Service

" Genesis Women’s and Child Care was founded on June 14, 2019 by Mrs.Chandrakala Magudapathi with a heartfelt mission — to provide quality,compassionate healthcare for women and children in the Periyanaickanpalayamcommunity. "

We began our journey opposite GKNM Hospital with just two consultationrooms, one waiting hall, two employees, and two dedicated doctors. From the very beginning, we focused on delivering personalized medical care in a welcoming and supportive environment. As our commitment and reputation grew, we expanded our services. On September 1, 2017, we opened Genesis Royal Infirmary in Puliyakulam, further extending our reach and ability to serve more patients with advanced facilities and specialized care. Today, Genesis stands as a trusted name in women’s and child healthcare, blending medical expertise, modern technology, and a compassionate touch — ensuring every patient feels valued, respected, and cared for.
